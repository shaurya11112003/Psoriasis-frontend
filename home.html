About SkinScan AI

SkinScan AI is an advanced web application designed to help identify psoriasis and other skin conditions using deep learning technology. Our goal is to make early skin diagnosis accessible, fast, and accurate for everyone.

Whether you're unsure about a patch on your skin or want to consult a dermatologist, SkinScan AI is here to assist you with AI-powered analysis and appointment booking.
